Computerized Pilot Selection System (CPSS)
We maintain, upgrade and provide technical support services for the Computerised Pilot Selection SystemÂ that is a jointly developed project by the Defence Institute of Psychological Research (DIPR) and the Aeronautical Development Establishment (ADE).
The systems consisting of 20 Nos. of Psychomotor systems and 100 Nos. of Cognitive systems are located at 3 Air Force Selection Boards at Mysore, Varanasi & Dehradun.
A state-of-the-art embedded micro controller based system, the CPSS with built-in security features has been indigenously designed and developed by DIPR & ADE against the sanctioned project of IAF.
The CPSS has psychomotor and cognitive tests. These tests comprehensively evaluate the qualities required for military pilots such as psychomotor skills, information processing skills (speed and accuracy), coordination, visualization and time-sharing by subjecting the candidates to perform concurrent multiple tasks.
