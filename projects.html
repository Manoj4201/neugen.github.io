PROJECTS

Our proficiency in user scenario mapping, hardware/software Integration, design and development, sub-system and system integration are at the core of our technology solutions portfolio with specific product solutions tailored for the defence and aerospace domain.
Lakshya - Pilotless Aircraft Ground Checkout System
This system is used to evaluate performance of the modules and sub modules of the Aircraft System. The software developed is used for ground check out during integration and flight trials.
Nirbhay Flight Line Test System - Fire Control Check out
Neugen developed the Health monitoring and ground checkout for validation of the Nirbhay missile fire control system. The software validates the fire control system before launch preparations.
Chandrayaan - Compiler Test Validation Suite
We conducted a compiler verification and validation activity for the ISAC - Chandryaan Project
Rustom II - Special Type Engine Test Equipment
Developed to operate in a laboratory for testing the Engine Instrumentation Unit of the Rustom II It simulates all inputs to the EIU, measures and receives all ouputs, records and displays the result. The data acquisition and control application was developed in LabVIEW

Mainly used for functional and performance tests during bench level , environmental qualification test and also during the HSI testing of the EIU
